Project directory

Ideas made inspectable, teachable, and occasionally playful.

Research frameworks, classroom resources, argument-analysis tools, creative experiments, and AI-assisted projects.

Every card opens the project's own site. This first-party directory gives search engines and readers a stable description of how the projects fit together.

Academic projects

Research and critical-thinking tools

CredencingA framework for assigning confidence in proportion to evidence, with uncertainty, defeaters, alternatives, and update conditions made explicit. ByteSeismicA philosophy and rationality hub for essays, tools, and projects around critical thinking, public reasoning, and AI-assisted inquiry. LogFallA fallacy reference built for diagnosis and argument repair rather than labels or gotchas. CogBiasA cognitive-bias guide mapping recurring distortions in perception, memory, motivation, and judgment. XiXteenCritical-thinking lessons and argument-analysis materials with structured prompts for reasoning practice.
