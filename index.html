01 Academic bio Public profile summary and working areas

Epistemology, credence, evidence standards.

Phil Stilwell is an academic consultant, researcher, and university instructor. Public profiles list him as a part-time lecturer at New York University School of Professional Studies, with profile affiliations including Soka University of Japan and the University of Tokyo.

His academic work focuses on rational confidence: how belief should track evidence, how inductive standards are applied, how public language handles partial belief, and how critical thinking can be taught without reducing disagreement to slogans.

Education

BA Philosophy, Summa Cum Laude, and MA Education, University of Kansas.

Research Areas

Epistemology, philosophy of science, induction, analytic philosophy, value theory.

Teaching

English, critical thinking, academic writing, technical writing, and macroeconomics.

Other Work

Futurology, AI-facing tools, public reasoning resources, and photo art.
