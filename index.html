Academic work first, with the wider life still in view.

Public profiles describe Phil Stilwell as an academic consultant, researcher, and university instructor whose career has centered on philosophy, epistemology, critical thinking, and macroeconomic theory, with more than two decades of professional work primarily in Japan.

General bio

Independent scholar of rational confidence and intellectual discipline.

Phil's academic center is epistemology: how confidence should answer to evidence, how inductive standards drift, how public language compresses uncertainty, and how reasoners can repair belief without turning disagreement into accusation. His work develops the credencing frame as a way to replace brittle knowledge-talk with graded, evidence-sensitive evaluation.

Profile anchors

  • Part-time lecturer, New York University School of Professional Studies.
  • Academia.edu profile lists Soka University of Japan and University of Tokyo affiliations.
  • BA Philosophy, Summa Cum Laude, and MA Education from the University of Kansas.
